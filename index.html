Actual Mental Health Resources

If you're struggling with mental health issues, here are some legitimate resources that can help:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 or 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • SAMHSA's National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
  • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) Helpline: 1-800-950-6264

Remember, seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Mental health conditions are real medical conditions that require and deserve proper treatment and support.
